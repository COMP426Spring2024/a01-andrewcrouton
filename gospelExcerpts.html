Central Pastafarian Beliefs

Pastafarians hold many core beliefs that describe wordly phenomena, the origins of Earth, and the Flying Spaghetti Monster.
  • Pirates are revered as "absolute divine beings" the original Pastafarians
  • Pastafarians tie the growing carbon dioxide concentration in the atmosphere in the past few hundred years with a strongly correlated decline in pirates across the world over the same time period.
  • Pastafarians attribute the stories of lost ships in the Bermuda Triangle to ghost pirates.
  • According to the Gospel, the negative descirptions of pirates as theives and thugs is Christian propaganda. Instead, pirates were well-meaning, morally respectable people who handed out candy to children.
  • To Pastafarians, the beer volcano is found in Heaven, wheras Hell consists of a beer volcano with stale beer.
